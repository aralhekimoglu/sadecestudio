About Us

Hi, we are Gamze and Aral — a husband-and-wife team based in Munich, Germany.

We started Sadece Studio because we were tired of apps that spy on you, confuse you, or nickel-and-dime you into a subscription you didn't want.

We are just two people building beautiful, privacy-first tools to help you take back control of your digital life. We build tools that prioritize beautiful design, intuitive simplicity, and absolute data sovereignty.

Our first release, Horizon, is a perfect example of this philosophy. We don't require accounts, we don't run analytics, and we don't touch your financial data. Everything stays encrypted in your own iCloud.

Horizon is our first app. It won't be our last.

Thank you for supporting indie software.

— Gamze & Aral
